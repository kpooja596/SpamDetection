About Project


This project addresses the problem of short messaging service(SMS) spam, that is classifying SMS according to the special keywords or by length of SMS or by the word count of the SMS into Spam message or Ham message. Under short messaging service (SMS) spam is understood the unsolicited or undesired messages received on mobile phones. These SMS spams constitute a veritable nuisance to the mobile subscribers. This marketing practice also worries service providers in view of the fact that it upsets their clients or even causes them lose subscribers. By way of mitigating this practice, researchers have proposed several solutions for the detection and filtering of SMS spam helped me out with their abilities. The main aim of this project is to classify the SMS into Spam or Ham and providing the online website platform for spam detection.
